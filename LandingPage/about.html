About Logan Business College

Founded in 1995, Logan Business College has established itself as a premier institution for business education, combining academic excellence with practical, hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for real-world success.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation needed to excel in today's dynamic business environment and become leaders who drive positive change in their organizations and communities.

With state-of-the-art facilities, industry-experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed in collaboration with top business leaders, we ensure our graduates are ready to make an immediate impact in their chosen careers.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence has made us one of the most respected business institutions in the region, with alumni working in Fortune 500 companies, successful startups, and leadership positions worldwide.

Our Full History

A legacy of excellence spanning nearly three decades

1995

The Beginning

Logan Business College was founded by Thomas Logan with just 50 students in a small building downtown. The vision was simple: provide practical, real-world business education that traditional universities weren't offering.

2000

First Accreditation

Received national accreditation and moved to our first dedicated campus. Launched our flagship MBA program, attracting students from across the country.

2005

Expansion Era

Opened our current main campus, doubling student capacity. Introduced specialized programs in Finance, Marketing, and International Business.

2010

Global Recognition

Named among the top 10 business schools in the region. Established international exchange programs with universities in Europe and Asia.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched our online learning platform, making business education accessible to students worldwide. Introduced cutting-edge programs in Digital Marketing and Data Analytics.

2020

Innovation Hub

Opened the Logan Innovation Hub — a state-of-the-art facility for entrepreneurship and startup incubation. Produced our first batch of successful startup founders.

2024

Today & Beyond

With over 2,500 graduates working at top companies worldwide, we continue to innovate and lead in business education. Our commitment to excellence remains stronger than ever.
