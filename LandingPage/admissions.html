Requirements

What you'll need to apply

Make sure you have the following documents ready before starting your application.

Completed Application Form

Fill out our online application with your personal and academic details.

Academic Transcripts

Official transcripts from your previous school or institution.

Personal Statement

A 500-word essay describing your goals and why you chose Logan BC.

Letters of Recommendation

Two letters from teachers or mentors who can speak to your abilities.

English Proficiency

TOEFL or IELTS scores for international applicants.

Valid ID

A government-issued identification document or passport.

Process

Admissions timeline

Follow these simple steps to join our community.

Step 1

Inquiry & Research

Explore our programs and reach out to our admissions team with any questions you may have.

Step 2

Submit Application

Step 3

Interview

Selected applicants will be invited for a brief virtual interview with our admissions panel.

Step 4

Decision & Enrollment

Receive your admission decision and confirm your enrollment to secure your spot.
