About Logan Business College

Founded in 1995, Logan Business College has established itself as a premier institution for business education, combining academic excellence with practical, hands-on learning experiences.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and ethical foundation needed to excel in today's dynamic business environment and become leaders who drive positive change in their organizations and communities.

2,500+ Graduates

Successful alumni working in top companies worldwide.

Expert Faculty

Industry professionals with real-world business experience.

95% Employment Rate

Graduates employed within 6 months of graduation.
